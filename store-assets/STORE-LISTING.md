### Full description (max 4000 chars)
```
Is your Android device too loud — even at the very lowest volume step?

Android only gives you ~15 hardware volume steps, and the jump between each one can be huge. On many phones and tablets, even step 1 above silent is still too loud for late-night listening, headphones, or a quiet room.

Granular Volume solves this with a floating, always-on-top control that adds 7 precise software sub-steps between any hardware level — so you can finally land on the volume that's just right.

──────────────────────────────
HOW IT WORKS
──────────────────────────────
Set your hardware volume to step 1 (or any step). Then use Granular Volume to fine-tune further: tap any of the 7 bars to move between 0 dB (no change) and -30 dB (very quiet). The control floats on top of any app — music, video, podcast, games — and stays there until you dismiss it.

──────────────────────────────
FEATURES
──────────────────────────────
▸ 7 precise levels — 0 to -30 dB, one tap each
▸ Floating control works on top of ANY app
▸ Drag it anywhere on screen — it remembers its position
▸ Up/down buttons for quick single-step adjustments
▸ Tiny and lightweight — barely visible until you need it
▸ Persistent notification shows the current attenuation level
▸ Works on Android 9 and newer

──────────────────────────────
PRIVACY — ZERO DATA, ZERO ADS
──────────────────────────────
▸ Completely free
▸ No advertisements — ever
▸ No internet permission — the app cannot connect to the internet at all
▸ No accounts, no sign-in, no tracking, no analytics
▸ Nothing leaves your device

──────────────────────────────
PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED
──────────────────────────────
• Display over other apps — required to show the floating volume control
• Modify audio settings — required to apply software-level attenuation

That's it. Two permissions, both explained, both necessary.

──────────────────────────────
PERFECT FOR
──────────────────────────────
Late-night listening · Sensitive ears · Quiet rooms · Headphone use ·
Anyone who's ever wished the volume could go just a little bit lower

Free, forever. Enjoy.
```
