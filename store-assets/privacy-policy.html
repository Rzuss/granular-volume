Privacy Policy — Granular Volume

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Granular Volume ("the app") is a utility that adds fine-grained, software-level volume steps on top of your Android device's built-in volume controls. Your privacy matters. This policy explains, plainly, what the app does and does not do with information.

The short version

Granular Volume does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet access — it does not request the INTERNET permission, contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs of any kind. Nothing ever leaves your device.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect names, email addresses, location, contacts, device identifiers, usage statistics, or any other personal or sensitive information.

Information stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of local settings on your device only, so it can remember your preferences between sessions:

  • The on-screen position of the floating control.
  • Your last selected volume-attenuation level.
  • Whether the control was active (used to optionally restart after reboot).

This data is stored privately in the app's own storage, is never transmitted anywhere, and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Permissions and why they are needed

  • Display over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) — to show the floating volume control on top of other apps.
  • Modify audio settings (MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS) — to apply the additional volume attenuation to audio output.
  • Foreground service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK) — to keep the control running reliably while you use other apps, with a persistent notification.
  • Run at startup (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — optional, to restore the control after the device restarts.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — to display the ongoing service notification required by Android.

None of these permissions are used to access, collect, or transmit personal information.

Children's privacy

The app collects no data from anyone, including children. It contains no ads and no in-app purchases.
