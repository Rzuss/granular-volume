Privacy Policy — Granular Volume

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Granular Volume ("the app") is a utility developed by an independent developer (Rotem Zussman, "the developer", "we") that adds fine-grained, software-level volume steps on top of your Android device's built-in volume controls. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with information, what rights you have, and the legal terms that apply to your use of the app.

The short version

Granular Volume does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet access — it does not request the INTERNET permission, contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs of any kind. Nothing ever leaves your device.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect, and have no technical means of collecting, names, email addresses, location, contacts, device identifiers, usage statistics, crash logs, or any other personal or sensitive information. We do not use cookies, SDKs, or any other tracking technology. We do not sell, rent, or share information about you, because none is ever collected in the first place.

Information stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of local settings on your device only, so it can remember your preferences between sessions:

  • The on-screen position of the floating control.
  • Your last selected volume-attenuation level.
  • Whether the control was active (used to optionally restart after reboot).

This data is stored privately in the app's own storage on your device, is never transmitted anywhere, is never accessible to the developer or any third party, and is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.

Permissions and why they are needed

  • Display over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) — to show the floating volume control on top of other apps.
  • Modify audio settings (MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS) — to apply the additional volume attenuation to audio output.
  • Foreground service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK) — to keep the control running reliably while you use other apps, with a persistent notification.
  • Run at startup (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — optional, to restore the control after the device restarts.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — to display the ongoing service notification required by Android for foreground services.

Every permission listed above is used exclusively for the on-device function described next to it. None of these permissions is used, or is technically capable of being used, to access, collect, or transmit personal information, since the app has no network access at all.

Third-party services

The app is distributed through Google Play and/or F-Droid. Distribution and installation are handled entirely by those platforms under their own respective privacy policies and terms, which apply to the download and update process itself, independently of anything described here. The app does not embed any other third-party service, library, or SDK that collects or transmits data of any kind.

Data security

Because the app collects no data and has no network access, there is no data-in-transit and no server-side storage to secure. The only information described above lives in your device's private, sandboxed app storage, protected by Android's own operating-system-level security model, and is never exposed to the developer.
