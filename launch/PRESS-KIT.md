# Granular Volume: Press Kit

For Android news sites, bloggers, and anyone writing about the app. Everything
here is free to use.

## One liner

Granular Volume is a free, open source Android app that adds the volume levels
your phone leaves out, below the hardware minimum, through a small floating dial.

## Short description

Sometimes the lowest volume setting is still too loud. Granular Volume adds fine
volume steps beneath Android's lowest hardware step, through a dial that floats
over any app. Drag it anywhere, close it with one tap. It does not override your
volume buttons or the system volume panel. Free, no ads, no tracking, GPL-3.0.

## Why it exists

On many phones the first volume step above mute is still too loud for a quiet room,
a sleeping baby, or a sensitive pair of headphones, and Android will not go lower.
Almost every volume app on the store goes the other way and makes things louder.
Granular Volume fills the underserved opposite: quieter than the minimum.

## What makes it different

- It goes quieter, not louder. The uncontested direction.
- Non invasive. No volume button override, no system UI takeover, so it stays stable.
- Single purpose. One dial, no equalizer maze, no account.
- Free, private, open source.

## Who it is for

Night listeners and parents, people with sensitive hearing, audiophiles with
sensitive IEMs and bone conduction headphones, and anyone in a quiet room.
